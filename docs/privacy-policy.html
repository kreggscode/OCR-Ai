OCR & Translation Toolbox - Protecting Your Privacy

Welcome to OCR & Translation Toolbox! We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Our extension processes images and text using AI technology while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. We believe in transparency, so we've made this policy clear and easy to understand.

üîç Information We Collect

Images You Upload

When you use the extension to extract text from images, we temporarily process the images you upload. Images are converted to base64 format and sent to Pollinations AI for OCR processing.

Text Data

The text extracted from your images and any text you choose to translate or simplify is sent to Pollinations AI for processing.

Technical Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Important: We do not collect your name, email, location, browsing history, or any personally identifiable information. Our extension works entirely offline except for API calls to process your content.

üéØ How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for providing the core functionality of the extension:

  • OCR Processing: To extract text from images using AI vision models
  • Translation Services: To translate extracted text between languages
  • Text Simplification: To make complex text more readable
  • Service Improvement: To enhance the extension's performance and features
  • Support: To respond to user questions and resolve issues

ü§ù Data Sharing and Third Parties

We partner with Pollinations AI to provide our OCR, translation, and simplification services. Here's how data sharing works:

Pollinations AI Integration

  • Your images are sent directly to Pollinations AI for processing
  • Extracted text is sent for translation/simplification
  • We do not store or retain your data
  • Data is processed securely via HTTPS

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs with our AI processing partner, which is necessary for the extension to function.

Pollinations AI Privacy: For more information about how Pollinations AI handles data, please visit their privacy policy at their official website.

üîê Data Security

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We implement multiple layers of protection:

  • No Local Storage: Images and text are never stored on your device
  • Secure Transmission: All data is sent via HTTPS encryption
  • Temporary Processing: Data is processed in real-time and not retained
  • Minimal Permissions: Extension requests only necessary access
  • Anonymous Usage: Any analytics data is completely anonymous

We regularly review and update our security measures to protect your data.

Your privacy is protected because we simply don't keep your information.
