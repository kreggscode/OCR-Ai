Privacy Policy for OCR & Translation Toolbox

Last updated: November 10, 2025

This privacy policy describes how OCR & Translation Toolbox ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Images You Upload: When you use the extension to extract text from images, we temporarily process the images you upload. Images are converted to base64 format and sent to Pollinations AI for OCR processing.

Text Data: The text extracted from your images and any text you choose to translate or simplify is sent to Pollinations AI for processing.

Technical Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide OCR (Optical Character Recognition) services
  • To perform text translation and simplification
  • To improve and maintain the extension
  • To respond to support requests

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use Pollinations AI APIs to process your images and text. Your data is sent directly to their servers for processing. We do not store, sell, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

Pollinations AI Privacy: Please refer to Pollinations AI's privacy policy at their website for details on how they handle data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • No local storage of images or text
  • Secure HTTPS communication with APIs
  • Images are processed temporarily and not retained
  • No user accounts or personal data collection

Data Retention

We do not store your images, extracted text, or any personal information. All processing is done in real-time and data is not retained on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the extension at any time
  • Request information about how your data is processed
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
