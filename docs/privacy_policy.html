Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

At OCR-AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from users. The extension processes images, PDFs, and text locally on your device for OCR and sends text data to Pollinations AI for translation and simplification.

Note: API calls to Pollinations AI may involve transmitting the extracted text to their servers for processing. We do not retain this data beyond the session.

Use of Information

Any text sent to Pollinations AI is solely for providing translation and simplification features within the extension. We do not use your data for any other purposes.

Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and do not store or share user data. All processing is done in real-time, and no data is retained on our servers.
