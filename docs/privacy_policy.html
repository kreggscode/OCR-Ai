This Privacy Policy describes how OCR & Translation Toolbox ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from users. The extension processes images, PDFs, and text locally on your device for OCR and sends text data to Pollinations AI for translation and simplification.

API calls to Pollinations AI may involve transmitting the extracted text to their servers for processing. We do not retain this data.

Use of Information

Any text sent to Pollinations AI is solely for providing translation and simplification features within the extension.

Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and do not store or share user data. All processing is done in real-time.
